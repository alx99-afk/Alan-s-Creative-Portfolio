About Me

Personal Story:

From a young age, I was fascinated by the power of visual storytelling. This passion led me to pursue a career in video editing, where I've honed my skills over eight years. During this time, I founded Artistic Features, a film production company, driven by my enthusiasm for the craft. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects ranging from YouTube videos to corporate productions.

Skills and Expertise:

  • Editing Software: DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Canva
  • 3D Software: Blender, Houdini
  • Specializations: Narrative-driven editing, color grading, motion graphics, sound design

Unique Selling Proposition (USP):

What sets me apart is my ability to blend creativity with technical precision, ensuring each video not only meets but exceeds client expectations.
